About Our Company

Feel the difference and Relaxation with Hotelwaze Company!

Hotelwaze aims to revolutionize the global tourism industry by providing a centralized, hassle free, easy access and secure advanced booking systems to both travel and tours and tourists who would avail the service.

The company has developed a mobile application that uses cashless transactions to eliminate scams and overpricing to international tourists and domestic travelers. This will also help migrant workers travel safely to and from their homes. The company also have plans to make local tourist transport compete globally by developing security measures that will ensure the safety of tourists.
